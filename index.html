AI in Medical Diagnostics

Context: Research paper written for CIS-199, Spring 2026. I chose this topic because I'm interested in how technology can make healthcare more accurate and accessible.

The topic I choose to do research on was how AI & Machine Learning could help with disease detection. There are different kinds of AI that could be used for diagnosing diseases and other health issues. The importance of AI and machines for healthcare is to help find the problem before making a final decision. The reason why AI and machines matter when it comes to healthcare is because one wrong decision could mean causing the initial problem to worsen.

There are many different machines used today to help with diagnosing issues with the patient. Some of these machines are x-rays, CTs, and ultrasounds. These machines are used for looking at the inside of a patient’s body to get a clearer picture of what is happening to them. These machines also detect how much of an issue the problem is to the patient.

Another kind of machine that can help diagnose issues with the body would be a glucose monitor. These monitors are used to check the patient’s sugar levels to see if they have any issues going on with their blood. The monitor will alert the patient if the blood sample has too much sugar or has too little. The patient can become sick if the level is too high and if it is too low.

Other types of AI being used by healthcare professionals are audio listening machines. The AI they use is called ambient AI to help with taking notes. The way this AI works is by listening to the conversation between the doctor and the patient and takes notes based on those conversations to save time with note taking. After the conversation is over, the doctor is able to edit the notes the AI took.

At Johns Hopkins University, researchers have developed an AI that helps determine which patients are at risk of getting sepsis. Many hospitals have this AI to catch the disease before it can take full root in the patient. This makes it easier to get treatment for sepsis before it can cause problems for the patient. Having this AI has saved many lives of patients that could have had the disease.

These machines and AI do have some problems depending on the kind of AI that is being used. The World Health Organization recommends not using ChatGPT and other AI tools that don’t give the best result to the problem. AI like ChatGPT are not meant for medical use and are prone to have errors in their results. Medical AI uses information about the patient, ChatGPT uses public information and nothing about medical records of the patient.

One of the major challenges that healthcare organizations faced was the COVID-19 pandemic. When the pandemic first happened, doctors and medical professionals were having trouble diagnosing the problem. Medical professionals had to start testing AI technology that was made to monitor people with COVID-19. That’s when they were able to develop a way to cure COVID-19 with the help of these AI machines.

Doctors believe that AI use in healthcare will be able to make many breakthroughs in the future. There are many opportunities that exist for AI powered machines. The potential of AI being trained to find anything wrong with a patient that a doctor missed or couldn’t see is a good opportunity for this technology. The possibility of being able to get rid of cancer for good is another opportunity that this technology is heading towards in the future.

In conclusion, AI and machine learning could help with medical checkups, disease detection, and other health issues. From my perspective, I think AI and machines have made things easier for us to figure out these health issues. Humans struggled to find the answers to certain kinds of diseases and viruses in the past. We still struggle to find a true way to make cancer a thing of the past.

Solar Energy Technology Innovations

Context: Research paper written for CIS-199, Spring 2026. I choose this topic because I'm interested in how solar energy is converted into electricity.

The topic I decided to research was solar energy technology innovations. Solar technology is used to convert sunlight into power. Technology like solar panels are placed on houses and fields to produce a lot of electricity. Due to how the cost of solar panels have dropped by 99% back in the 1970s, people have been using more solar panels as of recent years. It was all thanks to an MIT study that certain innovations caused the prices to be reduced.

The way that this kind of technology is being deployed is done in specific ways. Solar fields are a field of solar panels lined up in a row to convert a large amount of sunlight into electricity. Another way is by installing solar panels on the roofs of houses to give them their own energy conversion. These panels have helped renewable energy companies be able to grow and manufacture more ways of renewable energy using sunlight.

Other models of solar panels have been made to help in more urban areas. Lightweight and flexible models are among the model types that are used in these urban areas. This is because of their lightweight and it makes it easier to transport. The flexible models are used more on curved surfaces like roofs. This makes them even capable of being installed in wearable technology.

Another kind of solar technology is called off-grid power systems. Off-grid power systems are capable of producing electricity without having to be connected to a centralized network. These off-grid power systems usually have inverters to have direct power into alternate power. If there is no sunlight, lithium-ion, lead-acid, or flow batteries can also be used to power the grid.

Dragon Q Energy is a company that makes off-grid, solar-intergraded power systems. One of these off-grid power systems is called the PowerPole. The powerpole is able to scale according to certain needs while other units around the powerpole produce 15 kWh of energy. These powerpoles are also made to survive in extreme conditions like snowstorms. This allows them to be put in remote locations unlike other solar technologies.

There are a few issues that affect this technology whether it be the model of the cells in the panel or the energy conversion. When it comes to the models used today, it can affect the quality of the technology. Solar panels use solar cells to convert the sunlight into electricity. P-type photovoltaic cells were most commonly used for solar panels. Now a days, solar panels use the N-type photovoltaic cells for better efficiency making the P-types obsolete.

The other issue with the P-type photovoltaic cells was light-induced degradation. This makes the cells in the panels less efficient and convert less sunlight into electricity. If all of the panel’s cells are degradation, the panel will need to have all the cells replaced. The newer N-type photovoltaic cells are less susceptible to light-induced degradation and are better for long term usage.

Solar technology has been improving throughout the years. This technology has seen rapid technological advancements for how solar power is produced and stored. Solar farms like Bhadla Solar Park are able to produce over 2.2 GWs of electricity. Due to France’s push for solar adaptation, the DAS Solar’s manufacturing facility in Montbéliard is expected to increase local energy and jobs.

Solar panels have started to become intergraded into buildings more often in recent years. These panels are called solar shingles which helps with energy generation for the buildings. Perovskite-silicon tandem cells used to be limited to labs. They are now going to be put on the market with providing good performance at a low cost. Lithium iron phosphate batteries starting be more affordable which will help with solar storage.

In conclusion, solar technology has made major leaps in technological advancements ever since the 1970s. From my perspective, solar technology will at some point in the future be able to sustain us more than any other energy source. With all the more recent models of cells that the solar panels use, eventually we will have cells that are immune to degradation. However, this will depend on the materials used to make the cells.

Depending on the materials that are used to make these cells, it will either be better or work the same as previous cells. The N-type photovoltaic cells are a good example of upgrading from the previous models. This can also lead to making the upgraded models more fragile. Certain materials are better than others but one of those downgrades is the product becoming more fragile than the previous version.

Due to how more fragile certain technologies are becoming, this can affect the amount of time needed to finish the technology. This can cause delays and become an issue for the company that is making the technology. When a product is in higher demand, this will cause the product to get delayed or some to become faulty. This is why most companies that work with solar technology make mass produced versions of their most recent technology.

Another problem that this technology faces in the future is how much land the technology will need depending on how it’s deployed. Solar fields and farms need a large amount of land. Since these places are going to be having the panels convert lots of sunlight into electricity, having the correct amount of land is a necessity. These new technologies are providing electricity for cities and towns. In some areas, rental space is also used by homeowners renting portions of their land to the solar companies.

With all of these different kinds of solar technologies, each one has their own way of keeping them sustainable. Whether it be directly turning the sunlight into electricity or being self-sufficient. The solar companies that make these technologies are always trying to improve upon them to make their usage better for the future. I believe that one day, newer models will be able to convert moonlight and sunlight. This will make it so we will not have to worry about the solar technology shutting off or degrading with overusing the cells that are in them.

Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing

Context: Research paper written for CIS-199, Spring 2026. I choose this topic because I'm interested in how ethical hackers protect companies and organizations.

Ethical hacking and penetration testing are the ways that can help with fighting off malicious hackers. The main part of these two topics is that they work hand-in-hand together. This helps organizations and companies by attacking their weakest point to help reveal that weakness. This is to make sure that these exploits are found before a real hacker finds them.

Ethical hacking is not that different from the methods normal hackers use. Ethical hackers are also known as white-hat hackers. The malicious hackers are also known as black-hat hackers. The main difference between white-hat and black-hat hackers is that the white-hats have approval from the organization or company to hack into their systems. This helps with revealing weak points and vulnerabilities so they can be fixed before any problems arise in the future. As stated, the black-hat hackers are random people who cause malicious destruction of data and other important information.

Penetration testing is one of the methods that is used by an ethical hacker. A penetration test is mainly a mock cybersecurity attack to help with finding what areas need to be reinforced. Once the vulnerable area is found, the penetration tester will mimic how a malicious hacker would act to help with exploiting the vulnerability. This method is recommended by cybersecurity experts to help companies be more protected.

There are a few different kinds of penetration tests. Application pen testing target applications on the computer’s system. Network pen testing is used to target the company’s whole system. Hardware pen testing goes after devices that are connected to the network. The final penetration test is the personnel pen test which goes after the person using social engineering.

There are many risks when it comes to ethical hacking and penetration testing. Some of these risks are dangerous and could lead to major issues. One of these dangerous risks is the workforce gap with ethical hackers. This workforce gap is an issue because a prediction was made that by 2025, there will be a major shortage of cybersecurity professionals. The rise for ethical hackers was in very high demand during this time, with a prediction to reach 3.5 million.

Another one of these risks is the ever evolving cyberattacks that hackers develop. Due to the workforce gap with needing cybersecurity professionals, the number of black-hat hackers was outnumbering the white-hat hackers. The number of security breaches during 2025 was around 80% due to the lack of white-hat hackers. This meant that white-hat hackers had to learn some new skills to be able to match the black-hat hackers.

The future of ethical hacking and penetration testing looks to be heading in a good direction. As cyberattacks evolve and become more complex, ethical hackers had to learn the skills to counteract the complexities of these evolving cyberattacks. These newer skills could lead to better job opportunities. Some of these job opportunities are a Cloud Security Engineer and AI Security Specialist.

In conclusion, ethical hacking and penetration testing have become an important part for companies and organizations. From my perspective, ethical hackers are helping to deal with the issues that would cause major damage to companies and organizations. Usually when a black-hat hacker fails to complete their attack, they will try again later after upgrading their method of attack.

This could cause the black-hat hacker to find a different vulnerability in the system or is too much for the current method. Then it would require the white-hat hacker to learn how the attack happened and then improve upon their current systems and means of attack. This will then turn into a constant battle of improvement for both sides. That could lead to many companies requiring more ethical hackers to catch up with the number of attacks that could happen.

A good example of this would be how Nintendo has gotten hacked multiple times. One of these hacks was known as the Nintendo Giga Leak which started in March 2018 and ended on July 14, 2021. This leak started out small not causing any suspicion, but then it got out of hand. The leak was separated into multiple blog posts on 4chan containing all the leaked data.

There was a second leak known as the Teraleak focused more on Game Freak. This leak happened from 2024-2025 and was after assets from the Pokémon series. The data from this leak contained codenames for future games, source code, and a lot more relating to the series. However, some of the data that was leaked was personal information about some of the staff at Game Freak.

These cyberattacks caused major issues for Nintendo because a large amount of private information was leaked to the public. Attacks like this can cause major confusion in the company and make the employees feel unsafe. This could lead to the company losing so much profit and many customers. However in this case, Nintendo continued to move forward and improve their security.

The fact that Nintendo has been hacked multiple times is a major issue for both the company and their customers. Although Nintendo does get hacked quite often, they try to do the best they can when it comes to cybersecurity. Some improvements I can see that could fix this issue is by running at least one penetration test a month. This would help the employees find a quick solution to deal with the hackers.

This is why I think ethical hacking and penetration testing is beneficial. This protects the company and the customers from these kind of attacks when they happen. Since mostly everything is starting to move to digital, the overall ethical concerns are more prominent then they were before. Cyberattacks can cause so much damage to a company or organization which can leave a permanent impact on them. A company’s personal information is something that should only be known by the company and shouldn’t be public knowledge.

This is why companies are wanting more protection from these cyberattacks. The protection of their personal information is very important. The reason why is that it isn’t only the company’s personal information that is at stake. Their customer’s personal information is also vulnerable during these attacks. Which is why the rise of ethical hackers is very beneficial to help with this protection.
