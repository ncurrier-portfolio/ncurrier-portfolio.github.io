Solar Energy Technology Innovations

Context: Research paper written for CIS-199, Spring 2026. I choose this topic because I'm interested in how solar energy is converted into electricity.

The topic I decided to research was solar energy technology innovations. Solar technology is used to convert sunlight into power. Technology like solar panels are placed on houses and fields to produce a lot of electricity. Due to how the cost of solar panels have dropped by 99% back in the 1970s, people have been using more solar panels as of recent years. It was all thanks to an MIT study that certain innovations caused the prices to be reduced.

The way that this kind of technology is being deployed is done in specific ways. Solar fields are a field of solar panels lined up in a row to convert a large amount of sunlight into electricity. Another way is by installing solar panels on the roofs of houses to give them their own energy conversion. These panels have helped renewable energy companies be able to grow and manufacture more ways of renewable energy using sunlight.

Other models of solar panels have been made to help in more urban areas. Lightweight and flexible models are among the model types that are used in these urban areas. This is because of their lightweight and it makes it easier to transport. The flexible models are used more on curved surfaces like roofs. This makes them even capable of being installed in wearable technology.

Another kind of solar technology is called off-grid power systems. Off-grid power systems are capable of producing electricity without having to be connected to a centralized network. These off-grid power systems usually have inverters to have direct power into alternate power. If there is no sunlight, lithium-ion, lead-acid, or flow batteries can also be used to power the grid.

Dragon Q Energy is a company that makes off-grid, solar-intergraded power systems. One of these off-grid power systems is called the PowerPole. The powerpole is able to scale according to certain needs while other units around the powerpole produce 15 kWh of energy. These powerpoles are also made to survive in extreme conditions like snowstorms. This allows them to be put in remote locations unlike other solar technologies.

There are a few issues that affect this technology whether it be the model of the cells in the panel or the energy conversion. When it comes to the models used today, it can affect the quality of the technology. Solar panels use solar cells to convert the sunlight into electricity. P-type photovoltaic cells were most commonly used for solar panels. Now a days, solar panels use the N-type photovoltaic cells for better efficiency making the P-types obsolete.

The other issue with the P-type photovoltaic cells was light-induced degradation. This makes the cells in the panels less efficient and convert less sunlight into electricity. If all of the panel’s cells are degradation, the panel will need to have all the cells replaced. The newer N-type photovoltaic cells are less susceptible to light-induced degradation and are better for long term usage.

Solar technology has been improving throughout the years. This technology has seen rapid technological advancements for how solar power is produced and stored. Solar farms like Bhadla Solar Park are able to produce over 2.2 GWs of electricity. Due to France’s push for solar adaptation, the DAS Solar’s manufacturing facility in Montbéliard is expected to increase local energy and jobs.

Solar panels have started to become intergraded into buildings more often in recent years. These panels are called solar shingles which helps with energy generation for the buildings. Perovskite-silicon tandem cells used to be limited to labs. They are now going to be put on the market with providing good performance at a low cost. Lithium iron phosphate batteries starting be more affordable which will help with solar storage.

In conclusion, solar technology has made major leaps in technological advancements ever since the 1970s. From my perspective, solar technology will at some point in the future be able to sustain us more than any other energy source. With all the more recent models of cells that the solar panels use, eventually we will have cells that are immune to degradation. However, this will depend on the materials used to make the cells.

Depending on the materials that are used to make these cells, it will either be better or work the same as previous cells. The N-type photovoltaic cells are a good example of upgrading from the previous models. This can also lead to making the upgraded models more fragile. Certain materials are better than others but one of those downgrades is the product becoming more fragile than the previous version.

Due to how more fragile certain technologies are becoming, this can affect the amount of time needed to finish the technology. This can cause delays and become an issue for the company that is making the technology. When a product is in higher demand, this will cause the product to get delayed or some to become faulty. This is why most companies that work with solar technology make mass produced versions of their most recent technology.

Another problem that this technology faces in the future is how much land the technology will need depending on how it’s deployed. Solar fields and farms need a large amount of land. Since these places are going to be having the panels convert lots of sunlight into electricity, having the correct amount of land is a necessity. These new technologies are providing electricity for cities and towns. In some areas, rental space is also used by homeowners renting portions of their land to the solar companies.

With all of these different kinds of solar technologies, each one has their own way of keeping them sustainable. Whether it be directly turning the sunlight into electricity or being self-sufficient. The solar companies that make these technologies are always trying to improve upon them to make their usage better for the future. I believe that one day, newer models will be able to convert moonlight and sunlight. This will make it so we will not have to worry about the solar technology shutting off or degrading with overusing the cells that are in them.
